How to play

Buy streets, bleed rivals dry, and be the last mogul standing. Fast rounds, real players, zero downloads.

  1. 🎲

    Roll & move

    On your turn, roll the dice and advance. Hit doubles? Roll again — but three doubles in a row sends you to jail.

  2. 🏙️

    Buy the board

    Land on an open city? Snap it up. Every tile you own is another toll booth on someone else's vacation.

  3. 💸

    Collect rent

    When rivals land on your property, they pay you. The more you own, the louder their wallet cries.

  4. 🏗️

    Complete sets & build

    Own every tile in a color group? Rent doubles. Stack houses and hotels to turn a stroll into bankruptcy.

  5. 👑

    Rule the map

    Trade, auction, and outsmart the table. Bankrupt everyone else — or get sent home trying.

🏠🏨✈️

Start with $2,000 (classic rules). Pass GO for salary. Mortgages, auctions, and power cards spice up the grind.
